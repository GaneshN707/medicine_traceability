Medicine traceability

Medicine Traceability using blockchain is a concept that leverages blockchain technology to enhance the tracking and tracing of pharmaceutical products throughout the supply chain. Here's a description of how it works and its benefits:

Blockchain Technology:

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that enables secure, transparent, and immutable record-keeping. Each transaction or record is cryptographically linked to the previous one, forming a chain of blocks, hence the name blockchain.

Medicine Traceability:

Medicine traceability refers to the ability to track and trace pharmaceutical products as they move through the supply chain, from the manufacturer to the end consumer. It involves recording and monitoring the movement of medicines at each stage of the supply chain to ensure authenticity, prevent counterfeiting, and enhance safety and quality.
